A Motto

I think it's important to have guiding principles for your life. For me, that has been following Christ; I would highly recommend this route. Beyond that, I want to encapsulate my way of life into one sentence, an easily shareable, bite-sized manifesto. This is extremely reductive and somewhat stupid, but it's a good mental exercise and I think everyone should do it. Here's mine:

Think, and live.

It sounds overdramatic, but try this approach to living! It never fails.

I plan on eventually populating this part of my page with some reading recommendations and favorite websites, as well as some more extremely deep think-pieces.
